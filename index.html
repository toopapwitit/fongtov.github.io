The Problem

Many students and young professionals need good personal photos for LinkedIn, dating apps, social media, portfolios, or personal branding. However, traditional studio photoshoots can feel expensive, staged, and uncomfortable.

Our Solution

Fongtov Portraits offers relaxed outdoor portrait sessions in real urban environments. The goal is to create natural, confident, and authentic photos through a low-pressure street photography experience.

Benefits

  • Natural street-style portraits
  • Affordable sessions from around €50–60
  • Relaxed outdoor shooting atmosphere
  • Useful photos for LinkedIn, dating apps, social media, and portfolios
  • Easy booking through Calendly
