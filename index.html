01 / Problem Hypothesis

Good photos matter, but photoshoots often feel awkward.

Many students and young professionals need strong personal images for online profiles, applications, creative portfolios, and social platforms. However, traditional studio sessions can feel too formal, too expensive, and too uncomfortable for people who do not like posing.

Too staged

Studio portraits can look professional, but sometimes they feel stiff and unnatural.

Too expensive

Many young people want quality photos but do not want to pay premium studio prices.

Too much pressure

People often feel nervous in front of the camera because they do not know how to pose.

A relaxed street-style portrait experience.

Fongtov Portraits turns street photography into a bookable personal portrait service. Instead of forcing people into a studio setup, the session uses real urban backgrounds, natural light, and simple guidance to create confident, authentic images.
